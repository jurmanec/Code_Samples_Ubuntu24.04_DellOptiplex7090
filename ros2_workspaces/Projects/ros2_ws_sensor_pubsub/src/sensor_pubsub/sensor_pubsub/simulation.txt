
For Vibration (most important for civil inspection):

Normal operation: 0.2 – 1.5 (random walk around 0.8)
Occasional small events: spikes up to 2.5
Warning level: 3.0 – 4.5
Critical: 6.0+

For Temperature:

Normal: 18 – 32 °C (slow daily variation)
Warning: 40 – 48 °C
Critical: 55+ °C or sudden drops below 5 °C
